Our Story

A Legacy of Regal Taste

Nestled in the heart of Dhaka, The Royal Garden is more than a restaurant — it is an experience that honors tradition while embracing the extraordinary. Our bamboo-clad architecture, inspired by nature's own grandeur, sets the stage for an unforgettable evening.

Every dish is a love letter to heritage, crafted with age-old recipes reimagined through a regal lens. From our signature slow-cooked curries to our flame-kissed grills under open skies, we bring you flavors that linger long after the last bite.
